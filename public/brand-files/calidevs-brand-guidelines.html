calidevs. brand guidelines 07
Icon element
The Flame Dot

More than a dot

The period at the end of "calidevs." is our signature flame. It replaces the typographic dot and becomes the mark's identity — a small but unmistakable fire that says: we're always on.

  • Replaces the period — never use both a dot and a flame
  • Three-layer gradient: Ignite (outer), Amber (middle), Sand (core)
  • Can be used standalone as an app icon or favicon
  • Minimum size: 16px for digital, 5mm for print
  • The teardrop shape is organic — never make it perfectly round

calidevs. brand guidelines 16
Voice
How we sound

Direct

We say what we mean. Short sentences. Active voice. No corporate jargon, no buzzword bingo. If we can say it in 5 words, we don't use 20.

Confident

We know our craft. We speak with authority, not arrogance. We've built this before, and we'll build it again — better.

Warm

We're technical but human. We joke in Slack, we explain in plain English, and we care about the people behind the projects.

Do write

"Your store loads in 1.2 seconds. Here's how we got there."

"We hit a snag with the API. Already working on a fix — ETA 2 hours."

"Let's ship this."

Don't write

"We leverage cutting-edge solutions to synergize your digital transformation journey."

"Per our previous correspondence, please find attached the deliverables."

"We are excited to announce..."
